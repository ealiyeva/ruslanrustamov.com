My Biography

I am Ruslan Rustamov who was born on 8 March, year 1998 in Baku, Azerbaijan. I am Azerbaijani. This biography is the story of a man who was more than just a sportsman. I started my sport career at the age of 8. My sport type is boxing. Although I had huge responsibility of studying hard, I have never given up to continue my hard trainings. Therefore, I had been 13 times Azerbaijan champion. My first participation on international competition started with the city of Krasnodar, Russia. I had been a champion of this competition. My first great success was European championship in 2014 in Anapa, Russia. I am a champion of this Championship. In 2015 I was a champion of Europe in Kolobrzek, Poland. In 2016 I was granted with silver medal of European Championship in again Anapa, Russia. By considering the fact that I had several serious injuries from this competetions, 2 years I was a little bit far away from different international competetions. In 2021 I was granted with 3rd place in European Championship in Abruzzo, Italy. Currently I continue with my trainings, I strengthen my passion every day. My life goal is based on the Olympic Games in the year of 2024 and being champion of this competetion. Never forget my name RUSLAN RUSTAMOV. Because you will hear this name very often on international arenas of boxing...
